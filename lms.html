Class Management Software

Centralize attendance, communication, and scheduling

Why it works better than traditional school management tools

  • Real-Time SMS Notifications – Unlike app-based systems, SMS alerts ensure every parent and supervisor stays informed instantly.
  • Fingerprint-Based Attendance – Provides accurate, tamper-proof tracking for students and staff.
  • Integrated Teacher & Class Management – Combines scheduling, attendance, and class allocation in one system.
  • Comprehensive Communication Tools – From attendance alerts to SMS campaigns and birthday wishes, the system keeps everyone connected without extra effort.
  • All-in-One Solution – Reduces dependency on multiple platforms or manual tracking, making centre operations smoother and more transparent.

Save time on daily administration
